Internet of Things (IoT) Engineer

The Chief Technology Officer (CTO) is dedicated to pushing government technology forward to ensure that our systems can scale to future needs and deliver on the promise of Internet of Things (IoT), 5G, and other emerging technology. The Internet of Things (IoT) Engineer will play a lead role in all things IoT, ranging from design and requirements analysis to investigation of new technologies and software development. The successful candidate will engage regularly with senior managers, technologists, and the City’s customers to understand needs and vision and then translate this into an executable plan and roadmap for IoT. I


  Responsibilities

  • Provide technical leadership for the development and integration of new IoT technologies and systems
  • Partner with stakeholders to develop overall strategy, policy, and roadmaps for IoT in alignment with the Citywide CTO’s mission
  • Evaluate and prototype innovative technologies and concepts with IoT and related technologies
  • Help lead comprehensive technical studies, trials, and evaluations of new IoT-related infrastructure, and performance monitoring tools
  • Create policy and strategy for continuous modernization of IoT technology to meet the public’s evolving needs
  • Work with Citywide IT Security team to ensure that IoT technologies meet Citywide security standards and best practices
  • Handle special projects and initiatives as assigned


  Skills We're Seeking

Commitment to the mission of advancing greater equality and opportunity.

Design in any/all of the following practices: user experience, human centered design, interaction design, user research, data visualization, and service design.

Excitement about practicing human-centered design in government.

Background or interest in design, product development, public policy, health or social impact.

Turning user research insights into actionable insights and materials, such as user experience flows.

Excellent communication and presentation skills, both verbal and written.


  Education and Experience

Master's degree in computer science with 3 years experience in IT/computer programming.
