Join NYC Tech. Help us use technology to solve urban challenges.

We develop tech strategy and policies to make New York City the most tech-friendly and equitable city in the world. We need creative, purpose-driven people to join us.

We’re often hiring across the areas of broadband, smart technologies, digital product development, tech and internet policy, web and application development, and tech talent.

If you don’t see an opening that’s right for you at the moment, fill out our job interest form so we can keep you in mind. We also share tech and digital jobs from other City agencies in our monthly jobs roundup on Medium.


  Our Work

Our projects take different shapes and sizes but all center on the goal of improving quality of life for all New Yorkers.

Working with us might mean developing and implementing large-scale broadband infrastructure projects, partnering with local institutions to bring digital literacy programming to public housing, building user-centered digital products with City agencies, or working with local entrepreneurs and residents to prepare for a future with driverless cars.

Read about our projects in more detail on our website.
