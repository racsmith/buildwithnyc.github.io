Senior Futurist

The Senior Futurist will join NYC Tech's Industry Engagement team, which serves as the engine for bold ideas, breakthroughs and the way we interact and influence emerging technologies in the public realm. This position will prepare New York City's tech strategies for the future, collecting insights, identify patterns, design points of view, socialize concepts, and inform strategy for key projects and initiatives.

As Senior Futurist, you'll be responsible for research into the areas of NYC transportation, energy sustainability, zero waste, housing, autonomous vehicles, artificial intelligence, and drone technology, among others. You'll examine trends and breakthroughs in the context of the larger cultural, technological, economic, environmental, and political landscapes, screening for future threats as well as future opportunities.

In this role, you will examine how current and future citizens think, work, and live, by looking at external trends and drivers, and align these to civic needs, business needs, desired impact, technological drivers, and necessary internal next steps.


  Responsibilities

  • Research signals, drivers, and trends that may create uncertainty for Industry Engagement topic areas
  • Create a yearly “Moonshot” futures report which includes 10-year-out future scenario(s), drivers of change, gates and flags, synthesized elements to keep an eye on, and what this means for the city of New York
  • Work with the Industry Engagement team and external partners to translate foresight strategy into actionable next steps.
  • Monitor and track articulated gates and flags
  • Follow up with team progress to maintain momentum, assess, and adjust strategies
  • Provide effective direction and guidance for innovation by blending design with foresight tools
  • Develop and facilitate collaborative foresight workshops and working sessions


  Skills We're Seeking

Government experience, preferably at the municipal level

Results-oriented leadership experience

Experience developing and implementing technology projects from start to finish

Knowledge and experience working with emerging technologies

Ability to understand the strategic business objectives of the organization and translate a vision into strategies, goals, and plans

Ability to inspire and motivate staff at all levels and develop and maintain effective working relationships with diverse sets of partners in the tech and user community


  Education and Experience

Bachelor's degree and five years' experience of supervisory or administrative experience with a focus on business promotion or urban economic challenges
